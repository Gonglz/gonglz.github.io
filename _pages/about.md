I am Longzhao Gong, a Computer Engineering M.S. student focused on embedded AI, edge intelligence, and autonomous systems.

My current work centers on AI systems that leave the laptop and run closer to the physical world: small autonomous vehicles, sensor-driven activity recognition, model compression for constrained devices, and privacy-preserving machine learning systems.

This site collects my projects, technical notes, and writing. The goal is to make each project legible as an engineering story: the problem, the system architecture, the technical choices, the tradeoffs, and what I learned.

I also like the quieter side of technical work: explaining complex systems clearly, turning experiments into reusable notes, and making beginner-friendly material for people entering robotics and AI.

> In search of hidden passion, crossing barren lands once brushed by fleeting birds.
