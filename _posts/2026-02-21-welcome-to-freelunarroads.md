Hello, and welcome to **FreeLunarRoads** — my personal blog!

My name is Longzhao Gong, and this is where I'll be sharing thoughts on technology, life reflections, and the occasional trace of adventures along the way.

## Why "FreeLunarRoads"?

The name is a metaphor. The moon has no atmosphere, no pre-existing roads — just open terrain waiting to be crossed. That's how I like to approach new challenges: charting a course where none existed, guided by curiosity and persistence.

## What to Expect

- **Tech posts** — Deep dives into software, tools, and projects I'm working on.
- **Thoughts** — Short reflections on learning, growth, and ideas.
- **Traces** — Stories from places I've been and things I've experienced.

I'm glad you found this corner of the internet. Feel free to explore the [posts archive](/year-archive/) or browse by [category](/categories/) and [tag](/tags/).

Thanks for stopping by!
